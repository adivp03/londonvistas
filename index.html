How the fit score works. Fit = (Interest × 40%) + (Acclaim × 60%). Interest rewards overlap with history, politics, science, art, design and music (with your specific taste in Western classical, Indian classical, neo-classical, ambient, lo-fi and world music), and gives a lift to anything cross-cultural or non-Western. Acclaim starts from a standard host-prestige stand-in of 50 for anything not yet reviewed, and adds 10 for a genuinely good external review or canonical/masterwork status — mixed reviews stay at 50. Not-yet-reviewed events keep an informational flag (⚠) but are never scored down for it, and the strongest of them are gathered under strong fit, not yet reviewed at the foot of the page. Badges (Cross-cultural, Free, Closing soon, premieres, sold-out) are information only and never change the ranking.
