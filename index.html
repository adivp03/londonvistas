How the fit score works. Fit = (Interest × 40%) + (Acclaim × 60%). Interest rewards overlap with history, politics, science, art, design and music (with your specific taste in classical, Indian classical, neo-classical, ambient, lo-fi and world music), and gives a lift to anything cross-cultural. Acclaim starts from a standard host-prestige stand-in of 50 for anything not yet reviewed, and adds 10 for a good external review or canonical status — so strong critical reception nudges an event up without dominating. Not-yet-reviewed events keep an informational flag and are also gathered under strong fit, not yet reviewed at the foot of the page. Badges (Cross-cultural, Free, Closing soon, Premiere) are information only and never change the ranking.
